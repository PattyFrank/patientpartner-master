The Gap That Costs You the Most Patients

Most pharma patient support programs handle logistics well — prior auth, copay, enrollment, specialty pharmacy. Hubs have invested in these pillars for years.

But patients who have all the logistics handled still don't start therapy. The reason isn't access. It's fear, uncertainty, and the absence of someone who understands what they're going through.

This is the "missing middle" — and it's where Sections 3 and 4 of the scorecard will reveal your biggest opportunity.
