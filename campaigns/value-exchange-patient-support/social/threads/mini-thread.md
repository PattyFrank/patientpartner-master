# Threads Mini-Thread: Value Exchange

## Post 1

The transactional patient support model is breaking down.

Reimbursement assistance? Table stakes. Copay cards? Expected. Every major pharma brand has them.

So what actually differentiates?

## Post 2

The brands winning are asking a different question.

Not "How do we help patients access our therapy?"

But "What do patients need beyond our product—and where can we provide value?"

Specialist directories. Caregiver support. Workplace advocacy. Peer matching.

## Post 3

The data on peer-to-peer is hard to ignore.

68% more likely to begin treatment. 71% say mentorship was critical. 73% connection rate vs. 10–20% for traditional programs. 133.5 more days on therapy.

Fear, doubt, and lack of relatable support drive most drop-offs. Not prior auth. Not copay. The psychological barriers are the missing middle.

## Post 4

Bottom line: When clinical efficacy is comparable across competitors, patient support becomes the differentiator.

The brand that supports the broader health journey wins.

Peer-to-peer is how you deliver it.
