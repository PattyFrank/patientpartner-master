# Threads Post 01: Conversational Take

Something I've been thinking about that most pharma brands miss:

What do patients need from you that has nothing to do with your therapy?

The traditional answer—access assistance, copay cards, reimbursement—is table stakes now. Every major brand has it.

But patients managing chronic conditions need far more. Connection to specialists. Support for caregivers. Community. Someone who's been there.

The brands that asked differently saw 40% higher enrollment. 94% retention. Patients who said "this is the first pharma company that treated me like a whole person."

The shift is subtle. The impact is transformative.

What's your brand asking?
