# LinkedIn Text Post 01: The Right Question

**What do patients need from you that has nothing to do with your therapy?**

Most pharmaceutical brands never ask.

They start with the medication and work outward. Access assistance. Reimbursement support. Copay cards. The implicit contract: we help you afford our medication, you stay adherent.

That era is over.

A rare disease brand asked differently. What makes living with this disease difficult beyond the medication itself?

The answers had nothing to do with their therapy. Patients struggled to find specialists. They faced workplace discrimination. They felt isolated. Caregivers were burning out.

The brand built a program that addressed all of it. Specialist directories. Equipment grant connections. Workplace advocacy. Peer matching. Caregiver respite planning.

Patients enrolled at rates 40% higher than projected. They stayed engaged long after typical touchpoints would have ended. They referred other patients. They became advocates.

The patient feedback: "This is the first pharmaceutical company that treated me like a whole person, not just a prescription to fill."

That's the value exchange reimagined.

Not transactional support in exchange for adherence. Genuine partnership. Supporting entire health journeys. Earning trust through consistent presence.

The question you ask first changes everything.

What is your brand asking?
