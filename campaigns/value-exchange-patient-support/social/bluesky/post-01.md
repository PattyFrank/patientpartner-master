# Bluesky Post 01: Substantive Take

The value exchange in patient support is shifting. Transactional models (access assistance for adherence) are becoming obsolete—reimbursement and copay are table stakes. The brands reimagining support are asking: What do patients need beyond our therapy? Peer matching, caregiver support, specialist directories. The data: 68% more likely to begin treatment, 71% say mentorship was critical, 73% connection rate vs. 10–20% traditional. When clinical efficacy is comparable, patient support becomes the differentiator.
