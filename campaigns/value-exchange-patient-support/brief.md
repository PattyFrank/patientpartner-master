# Extraction Brief: Value Exchange Patient Support

## Core Insight

Leading pharma brands are reimagining patient support from transactional (access assistance for adherence) to relational (support entire health journeys). Peer-to-peer mentorship is the operational backbone of this shift—and the data proves it.

## Supporting Points

1. **The transactional model is obsolete** — Reimbursement assistance and copay cards are table stakes. Patients need far more: connection to specialists, caregiver support, community, someone who's been there.

2. **The right question changes everything** — Instead of "How do we help patients access our therapy?" ask "What do patients need beyond our product?"

3. **Rare disease case study** — A brand that built specialist directories, peer matching, workplace advocacy, and caregiver respite saw 40% higher enrollment and patients who became advocates because "they treated me like a whole person."

4. **Peer matching is the operational solution** — Fear, doubt, and lack of relatable support drive most drop-offs. Hubs solve operational barriers. The psychological barriers are the missing middle. Peer-to-peer solves them.

5. **The data backs it up** — 68% more likely to begin treatment. 71% report mentorship as critical. 73% connection rate (vs. 10–20% traditional). 90% confidence boost. 133.5 more days on therapy.

6. **When clinical efficacy is comparable, patient support becomes the differentiator** — The brand that supports the broader health journey wins.

## Stories/Examples

- Rare disease brand: specialist directories, equipment grants, workplace advocacy, peer matching, caregiver respite → 40% higher enrollment
- Patient quote: "This is the first pharmaceutical company that treated me like a whole person, not just a prescription to fill."

## Data/Proof

- 71% report mentorship as critical to decision
