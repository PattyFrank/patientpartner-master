When Hesitation Is Highest, Who Connects Fastest?

Patients drop off in the gap between "approved" and "first dose." The speed of peer connection at that moment is the single most important variable in converting hesitation into action.

PatientPartner matches patients in real time based on disease state, treatment experience, and demographics. Mentors respond same day. No coordinator overhead, no scheduling friction.

Snow Companies relies on Mentor Program Specialists who "coordinate schedules for Mentees/Mentors." Average lag: 3–7 days to first connection. The hesitation window compounds with every passing day.

Reverba targets a 3-touch cadence within 7–14 days of signup. Conversion degrades as complex onboarding creates dropout before the first call even happens.

Internal programs depend entirely on team bandwidth. Speed and quality degrade as programs scale and as key operators leave.
