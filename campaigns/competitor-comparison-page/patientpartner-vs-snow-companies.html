Where PatientPartner Wins

Four dimensions where a SaaS platform fundamentally outperforms the agency model.

Speed: Real-Time vs. Coordinator-Scheduled

Patients are most likely to abandon treatment in the gap between approval and first dose. The speed of peer connection at that moment is the most important variable in converting hesitation to action.

PatientPartner matches patients in real time based on disease state, treatment experience, and demographics. Mentors respond same day. No coordinator overhead. No scheduling friction.

Snow uses Mentor Program Specialists who "coordinate schedules for Mentees/Mentors" and conduct "post-call evaluations" (from their own job descriptions). Patients wait 3–7 days. The patient's moment of highest hesitation passes.

Outcome Attribution: ROI Dashboard vs. Modeled Activity Metrics

Snow delivers monthly or quarterly reports from Salesforce exports and presentations. ROI is modeled retrospectively using lifetime value assumptions. Results are estimated at 3:1 to 4:1, based on self-reported or third-party validation.
