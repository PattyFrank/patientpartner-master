# Campaign Brief: The Peer-to-Peer Playbook

## Asset
**The Pharma Leader's Playbook: How Peer-to-Peer Mentorship Outperforms Every Other Patient Engagement Tactic**

## Format
Mini-guide / framework. 6 chapters, ~14 pages. Designed HTML (print to PDF). Standalone landing page.

## Goal
Position PatientPartner as the category authority on peer-to-peer patient engagement. Generate qualified leads (Director+ at pharma, med-tech, clinical trial divisions) through thought leadership that delivers genuine value before asking for anything.

## Hook
"The framework behind 68% more treatment starts and 133.5 more days on therapy."

## What Makes This Asset Valuable
Unlike the scorecard (self-assessment tool), this playbook is a **data-rich authority piece**. It synthesizes:
- 16 external sources (IQVIA, Edelman, Health Union, Frontiers, Accenture, Pharmaceutical Commerce)
- PatientPartner's own 12-month study data (263 patients, 193 connections, 48 mentors)
- Real adherence economics ($100–$300B cost of non-adherence, 125,000 annual deaths)
- Head-to-head comparison of 9 intervention types
- An actionable framework (5 principles) and implementation roadmap (16 weeks)
- ROI model with specific dollar figures

The playbook teaches the principles. PatientPartner is the platform that makes them operationally possible at enterprise scale.
