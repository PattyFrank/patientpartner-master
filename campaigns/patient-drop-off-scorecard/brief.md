# Campaign: Patient Drop-Off Scorecard

## Goal
Generate high-intent leads (Director+ at pharma, med-tech, CROs) via a self-diagnostic scorecard that reveals gaps in patient support programs — specifically the "missing middle" between enrollment and therapy start. Target: 200+ gated downloads in first 60 days across LinkedIn ads, organic email, and website conversion.

## Format
Scorecard / Self-Assessment (PDF, 7 sections, 35 items, scored 1–5, with interpretation guide and business case framework)

## Hook
"Score your patient support program in 5 minutes. Find the gaps that cost you patients."

Alternate hooks for testing:
- "Pharma invests $22B+ in patient support. Only 3–8% of patients use it. Where's your program leaking?"
- "The Patient Drop-Off Scorecard: 35 questions that reveal where your patients disappear."
- "Your patients enrolled. They didn't start. This scorecard shows you why."

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Director of Patient Services, VP Patient Support, Director Patient Engagement at pharma companies (Gilead, Lundbeck, Ipsen, Teva, Biogen, Covis, Sobi)
- **Secondary:** Director Patient Engagement / Product Marketing at med-tech (Medtronic, Boston Scientific, Insulet, Mainstay Medical)
- **Tertiary:** Director Clinical Operations, Head of Recruitment & Retention at CROs and pharma clinical divisions

## Bridge to Paid Offer
The scorecard is designed so that Sections 3 (Emotional Readiness) and 4 (Peer Connection) are the most common weak points. These sections reveal the exact problem PatientPartner solves. The interpretation guide makes the connection explicit: if you score low in these areas, peer mentorship is the highest-ROI intervention — and PatientPartner is the platform that delivers it at enterprise scale.

## Paid Offer
PatientPartner Platform — enterprise SaaS for peer-to-peer patient mentorship. Annual contracts, enterprise pricing. Platform includes real-time mentor matching, sentiment analytics, compliance infrastructure, and ROI reporting.

## Competitive Differentiation
Every competitor lead magnet in this space (IQVIA, ExtendMed, Guidehouse, AssistRx, Momentum LS) is a static PDF report or whitepaper. Nobody is offering a self-diagnostic tool. This scorecard is:
- **Actionable** — a tool, not a read
- **Shareable** — designed for team scoring and internal discussion
- **Recurring** — meant to be re-scored quarterly, keeping PatientPartner top of mind
- **Revealing** — built to surface the "missing middle" that most programs overlook
