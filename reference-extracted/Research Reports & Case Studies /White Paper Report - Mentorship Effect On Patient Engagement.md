**Study Overview**


**Scope**


The study tracked 263 prospective patients as they navigated treatment decisions. The PatientPartner
platform was used to match these patients with mentors who had previously experienced similar health
challenges.


**Methodology**


Data was collected over **12 months**, utilizing both quantitative metrics (connection rates, progression
steps) and qualitative patient feedback through post-connection surveys.


**Key performance indicators included:**


**Connection Rate:** 193 out of 263 prospective patients **73%** successfully established one-to-one

connections with mentors.


**Impact on Treatment Decisions:** Among those connected, **71%** of patients reported that the mentor

interaction was influential in their decision to move forward with treatment.


**Progression in Healthcare Journey:** For all patients who had a mentor connection, **68%** of them
took proactive next steps (such as requesting a physician referral, scheduling a treatment




**Key Findings**


**1. High Connection Success:**
The platform achieved a 73% connection rate, significantly exceeding the typical 10–20% engagement
seen in traditional patient support programs. This high rate underscores PatientPartner’s effectiveness in
facilitating meaningful peer-to-peer connections.


**2.** **Influence on Treatment Decisions:**

A remarkable 71% of patients indicated that their mentor connection was a decisive factor in choosing to
proceed with treatment. This demonstrates that real-time, one-to-one mentorship can overcome patient
hesitancy by providing firsthand, relatable insights into treatment options.


**3.** **Encouraging Proactive Patient Behavior:**
With 68% of connected patients taking the next step in their healthcare journey – whether through
scheduling appointments, seeking referrals, or requesting prescription support – the data confirms that
mentorship not only informs patients but also drives actionable outcomes.


**4.** **Qualitative Benefits:**

In addition to the quantitative metrics, qualitative feedback highlighted several recurring benefits:


**Reduced Anxiety:** Patients reported lower levels of anxiety about treatment options after engaging
with a mentor.


**Enhanced Confidence:** Many felt more empowered and confident in their healthcare decisions.


**Increased Understanding:** Mentorship provided clear, practical insights into what to expect from
various treatments.
