# **Case Study: Patient Recruitment for a** **Phase II Neurodegenerative Trial**

## **Background and Context**

Recruitment for neurodegenerative disease trials presents a distinct set of challenges that extend well
beyond lead generation. Patients and caregivers often face heightened emotional burden, uncertainty
around diagnosis and progression, and hesitation about trial participation driven by fear of the unknown. As a
result, initial expressions of interest frequently fail to translate into screened referrals at the site level.


In this program, PatientPartner partnered with a third-party digital marketing agency responsible for
generating patient interest and capturing inbound leads. While lead volume was strong, the sponsor
identified a significant drop-off between initial interest and site referral. The primary need was not additional
awareness, but a structured, compliant mechanism to convert motivated but uncertain patients into
informed trial participants.


PatientPartner was engaged specifically to address this conversion gap.


## **Our Approach**

PatientPartner’s role was deliberately narrow
and execution-focused. We worked downstream

of lead generation and upstream of site referral.
All patients entering the program had already
expressed interest in trial participation through
the marketing partner.


Our objective was to reduce patient hesitation,
increase readiness for trial participation, and
facilitate qualified referrals to participating sites
without adding operational burden to site staff.


The strategy centered on peer-to-peer
mentorship, delivered at the moment of highest
patient uncertainty. By introducing patients to
trained mentors with lived experience, we
helped translate abstract trial information into
practical, experience-based understanding
that supported confident decision-making.

improved recruitment efficiency and lead

insufficient. Experience-based peer support can be a decisive factor in converting interest into action when
applied at the right moment in the recruitment funnel.
