  - **VP of Patient Marketing**

      - _Leads the overall patient marketing strategy, overseeing campaigns to engage_

# **Additional Relevant Titles**


- **Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)**

    - _Oversees all marketing efforts, developing strategies to promote products and_

_engage patients._

- **Chief Patient Officer**


    - _Leads patient-centric strategies across the organization, ensuring patient needs_

_are prioritized._

- **Head of Patient Centricity**

    - _Directs initiatives to embed patient-focused approaches in all company_

_operations._

- **Director of Patient Outcomes**


    - _Manages programs aimed at improving patient health outcomes through various_


_interventions._

- **Director of Therapy Area Marketing**

    - _Leads marketing strategies for specific therapeutic areas, tailoring approaches to_

_patient needs._

- **Patient Journey Manager**

    - _Maps and analyzes the patient journey to identify opportunities for improvement._

- **Director of Multichannel Marketing**

    - _Oversees marketing across multiple channels to reach patients effectively._

- **Director of Oncology Marketing (or other specific therapeutic areas)**

    - _Leads marketing efforts for oncology products, focusing on patient engagement_


_and education._

- **Director of Patient Loyalty Programs**

    - _Develops programs to increase patient retention and loyalty to treatments._

- **Patient Communications Manager**

    - _Manages communication strategies to effectively inform and engage patients._

- **Director of Stakeholder Engagement**

    - _Oversees engagement with key stakeholders, including patients, providers, and_

_advocacy groups._
