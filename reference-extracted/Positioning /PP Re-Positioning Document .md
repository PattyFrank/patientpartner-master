### **PatientPartner: Market Positioning and Core ICP Strategy Outline**

**1. Overview of PatientPartner’s Position in the Market**

## Positioning Statement : PatientPartner’s Mentor Connect Annual Subscription is

positioned as a next-generation patient engagement platform specifically designed for
enterprise pharmaceutical companies, and med-tech companies. The platform bridges a gap in
traditional patient support systems by delivering personalized, real-time mentorship for patients
considering or beginning complex therapies or surgical intervention. By aligning patient
experience with digital transformation, and an industry leading regulatory-compliant technology,
PatientPartner stands at the forefront of modern patient engagement, addressing core
challenges in adoption, adherence, support, and satisfaction.

## **Unique Selling Proposition:**

PatientPartner uniquely delivers **on-demand, patient-mentor matching and real-time**
**engagement**, setting it apart from standard patient advocacy or customer support models.
Offered as a **white-labeled solution** for both treatment lines and surgical interventions,
PatientPartner’s platform becomes an integral part of each client’s brand, enhancing their
engagement strategies with seamless, branded support.


What truly differentiates PatientPartner is our **data-driven insights and patient sentiment**
**reporting** . By capturing anonymized patient sentiment through each interaction, we provide our
clients with a powerful layer of analytics that goes beyond traditional engagement metrics. This
data offers a detailed understanding of patients’ emotions, concerns, and motivations as they
journey through treatment, providing actionable insights that are invaluable for enterprise
pharma companies.


With patient sentiment reporting, our clients can:


  - **Identify engagement pain points early**, allowing for timely interventions to prevent

patient drop-off.

  - **Adapt messaging and support protocols** based on real-time patient feedback,

aligning communication strategies with the evolving needs of patients.

  - **Gain a nuanced view of treatment adherence challenges** that helps tailor patient

engagement, marketing, and support efforts in a way that resonates authentically with
patients.


In a value-driven healthcare landscape, these insights enable enterprise pharma companies to
**refine their engagement strategies** with precision, resulting in improved adherence, patient
satisfaction, and measurable ROI. PatientPartner not only empowers clients to understand
patient needs more deeply but also equips them with the tools to respond proactively, ensuring
that patient journeys are supported, personalized, and impactful at every step.


## **Compliance & Regulatory Standards:**

As an industry compliance leader, PatientPartner is certified and exceeds industry standards in
correlation with HIPAA, SOC 2 and ISO 27001 standards, allowing us to operate seamlessly
within the highest levels of enterprise requirements. This robust commitment to data security,
privacy, and regulatory standards gives our enterprise clients confidence in a secure and
compliant patient engagement solution that aligns with the strictest industry mandates.

## **Integration & Onboarding Excellence:**

PatientPartner’s **Enterprise Integration Team** ensures a smooth, efficient transition for clients
moving from existing patient engagement providers or internal programs to our advanced
Mentor Connect platform. Our dedicated team conducts **on-site integration sprints** to
minimize transition costs and streamline implementation. This hands-on approach guarantees
the platform’s quick, efficient launch and ensures that every aspect of the setup aligns with the
client’s unique needs.

## **Tailored Training for Patient Engagement Success:**

PatientPartner not only integrates seamlessly with existing infrastructure but also invests in the
long-term success of each client’s patient engagement program. Our team provides
**comprehensive training for all members involved in patient engagement** to ensure they
are fully equipped to maximize the platform’s potential. We focus on empowering each client’s
team with the tools and knowledge needed to create meaningful, lasting patient connections.

## **Augmented Support with Patient Engagement Managers:**

To further support client success, PatientPartner offers **Patient Engagement Managers** who
work as an extension of the client’s internal team. These managers oversee the mentor
population, supporting day-to-day engagement and delivering insights to continually refine
patient strategies. Through this partnership, clients benefit from a **99% mentor retention and**
**satisfaction rate**, ensuring a consistent, high-quality experience for patients. This level of
support provides peace of mind, allowing clients to focus on strategic initiatives while we
maintain the high standard of patient care that drives engagement and adherence.

the personalization, immediacy, and data analytics required to support complex, high-stakes
therapies. Patients often feel isolated or confused, leading to low adoption to new treatments,

patients receive relatable, emotional and practical support from trained mentors, increasing
