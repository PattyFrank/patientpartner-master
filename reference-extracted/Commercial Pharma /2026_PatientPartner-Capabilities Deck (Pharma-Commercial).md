#### How it Works

**PatientPartner** turns brand.com traic into real patient action through
fast, compliant, and trusted peer mentorship.







1. **Incoming Patient**

A patient struggling with a condition seeks trusted
resources and treatment options.


2. **Mentor Match**


The patient is matched with a trained mentor who
has lived experience with the same condition.


3. **Treatment Insights**

Through real conversations, the patient learns
about relevant treatment options and what to
expect.


4. **Behavior Change**

With renewed confidence, the patient takes
action—whether it’s booking a consult, refilling a
prescription, or asking their doctor about a new
therapy.


5. **Personalized Treatment Pathways**

The patient either initiates a new prescription or
remains adherent to their existing treatment,
based on their needs and goals.


6. **Progress**

The patient improves their health or continues
their treatment journey with increased confidence
and consistency.


7. **Patient Becomes Mentor**


Inspired by their own experience, the patient
returns to the platform to help guide others
through similar journeys.
