# **How It Works**

##### **Trained on Real Conversations**

**Built from 200M+ anonymized real-world patient**


**interactions, the Perfect Patient Avatar captures authentic**


**tone, empathy, and insights proven to resonate, engage, and**


**convert patients to action.**

##### **Brand-Specific Knowledge Base**


**Every avatar is uniquely configured around your brand’s**


**specific treatment protocols, prescribing information, and**


**medical guidelines. Ensuring precision, regulatory alignment,**


**and fully compliant patient interactions at every touchpoint.**

##### **Deployed Across Key Channels**


**Seamlessly integrates across patient hubs, CRMs, SMS,**


**patient portals, apps, and third-party platforms, meeting your**


**patients exactly where they are. Always accessible, to ensure**


**your patients never feel alone.**

##### **Continuously Monitored**


**All nteractions are monitored, logged, and refined**


**continuously—enhancing engagement, adherence, and**


**patient satisfaction, while ensuring comprehensive AE/PC**


**oversight.**


#### **What is the Perfect Patient?**

The PerfectPatient is a next-generation conversational


agent designed to **replicate the empathy, insight, and**


**personal experiences of real patient mentors.** Available


24/7 and able to handle over 1,000 conversations at the


same time.


**Trained on anonymized transcripts from real patient-**


**mentor conversations**, the AI mentor delivers high-touch,


personalized guidance that **supports patients from pre-**


**diagnosis through treatment initiation and adherence** .

##### **Seamless Integration**


**Our team fully manages the entire rollout—from configuration**


**to launch—in just 4–6 weeks, ensuring a smooth deployment**


**with minimal effort required from your internal teams.**

##### **Unparalleled Reporting**


**Every patient interaction is automatically transcribed &**


**summarized, highlighting critical insights and key discussion**


**points. Unlocking real-time patient sentiment, emotional**


**trends, and predictive adherence indicators—giving you**


**unprecedented visibility into patient behavior and outcomes.**
