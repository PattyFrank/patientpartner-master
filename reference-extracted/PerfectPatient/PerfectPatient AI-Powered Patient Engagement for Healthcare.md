###### **2. What Is PerfectPatient Mentor?**

PerfectPatient Mentor is our answer to the growing demand for personalized, real-time patient
support at scale. It is an AI-powered patient engagement platform designed for pharmaceutical,
med-tech, and healthcare organizations to connect with patients in a more authentic and
effective way. In simple terms, PerfectPatient acts as a virtual “mentor” for patients, providing
instant information, guidance, and encouragement that is tailored to each patient’s needs. This
solution was built on the foundation of PatientPartner’s nearly decade-long experience in
patient mentoring programs, but instead of relying solely on human mentor matches, it
harnesses artificial intelligence to reach every patient with the right support at the right time.

**The Current Landscape**


Below is a snapshot of the main categories shaping the AI-in-healthcare race. Each
demonstrates how quickly this technology is being operationalized, and how much opportunity
exists for smarter, compliant patient engagement.


**1** **Patient Outreach Agents (Voice/Chat)**


Safety-focused “healthcare agents” are already managing non-diagnostic tasks like
post-discharge follow-ups, chronic care check-ins, and appointment reminders.
Platforms such as Hippocratic AI are proving that patients will engage with AI when it’s
trustworthy, empathetic, and easy to use.


**2** **Brand & Journey Platforms (Life Sciences)**


Tools like **Ostro Health** are helping pharma and med-tech brands personalize outreach
through AI-driven journeys across email, SMS, and web. These solutions are effective in
scaling education and engagement—especially when built around pre-approved, MLRcompliant content.


**3** **Contact Center & Access Automation (Providers)**


Platforms such as Hyro and Syllable use conversational AI to deflect inbound calls,
manage scheduling, and streamline basic patient inquiries. They deliver operational
efficiency but focus primarily on logistics, not longitudinal engagement.


**4** **Care Navigation & Triage**


Solutions like **Clearstep** and **Ada Health** guide patients to the right care setting based
on symptom inputs, helping hospitals reduce unnecessary visits and improve routing

accuracy.


**5** **Clinician Productivity & Workflow Tools**


AI-powered transcription and ambient documentation solutions—Abridge at Kaiser
Permanente or Microsoft’s integration with Epic Systems—are now standardizing
generative AI in clinical settings. These tools don’t interact directly with patients but
represent a major shift in operational efficiency and clinical readiness.

**Clinical Trials: From Bottleneck to Breakthrough**


Few areas stand to benefit more from AI than clinical research. Trials are often the most

expensive and time-consuming part of bringing a therapy to market, and the hardest to optimize.


The market for **AI in clinical trials is growing nearly 19% annually**, projected to rise from $7.7 B
in 2024 to $21.8 B by 2030 (ClinicalTrialRisk.org). That growth is driven by AI’s ability to fix longstanding pain points:


**Faster patient recruitment:** AI can mine electronic health records, claims data, and social
listening tools to match eligible participants more precisely, addressing the 37% of trial
delays caused by enrollment struggles.


**Smarter trial design:** Predictive modeling enables virtual simulations and adaptive protocols
that reduce costs and improve study efficiency.
